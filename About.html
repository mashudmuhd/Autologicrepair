About

The Logic Auto Repair Workshop (LAR) is an integrated Automobile Workshop in Mussafah, Abu Dhabi, providing Preventive, Corrective and Predictive Maintenance services for light, medium and heavy vehicles. It started off as a small workshop in 2017 and has now grown into an organization through hard work and dedication.

LAR provides quality service in Automobile Maintenance that is widely recognized in the market. The Internal Quality Assurance guidelines are maintained with minimum deviations thus making LAR one of the established workshops in the region. The geniality of the staff and superiority of service at LAR has enabled customers to trust their vehicles in our hands. This has facilitated the building of long term relationships with several of our clients that include government organizations, multinational companies and individual customers.

LAR comprises of a specialized team of Service Advisor, Mechanics, Denters/Panel beaters, Spray Painters, A/C Mechanics & Auto Electricians that are competent and well trained. Our technical personnel have had multi-dimensional “Hands-on” experience in the maintenance of a wide variety of vehicles, petrol or diesel, which include and are not limited to American, European, Japanese, and Korean. The LAR base and workshop facilities in Mussafah M6 over an area of 5,000 sq. ft. The workshop is well equipped with modern equipments and tools to execute a vehicle output of approximately vehicles per month.RARW
